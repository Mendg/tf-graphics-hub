How to use these graphics

  1. Open a graphic page from the cards below.
  2. Click any orange-outlined text to edit it — put in your name, miles, goal, whatever fits.
  3. Screenshot (or use Cmd+Shift+4 on Mac / Win+Shift+S on Windows) to save it.
  4. Post to Instagram Stories, Facebook, WhatsApp — tag friends and drop your fundraiser link.
